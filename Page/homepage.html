Cinema is a composite art form combined with images and sounds, sometimes with some other form of sensory stimulation; stored on some form of video recording device for dissemination to the public through various technical means: theater, television, web/stream, video, tape, disc, projector...

Previously in Vietnamese , based on the form of popularization or distribution that cinema films were called " plastic films " or " screen films " , to distinguish them from video films ; but with the development of technology, this calling is no longer appropriate, because a movie can be shown on many different devices.

Currently, Cinema Films are films produced to be shown mainly in cinemas, with new technology the films can be recorded with film or digital devices. Movies are divided into many genres, after being released in theaters, movies will be popularized in many forms: television, online services or physical products: VHS, DVD, Bluray...
